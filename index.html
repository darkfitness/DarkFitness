Diet

Follow a healthy diet to burn fat and grow muscles.

  • Breakfast: Oats with fruits, 2 roti and 1bowl daal, boiled eggs or protein shake
  • Mid-morning: Nuts (almonds, walnuts) or Greek yogurt
  • Lunch: Brown rice / Quinoa + Chicken / Paneer + Salad
  • Snack: Smoothie or fruit
  • Dinner: 1 roti and 1bowl daal or green vegetables, Grilled fish / Paneer / Chicken + Veggies
  • Hydration: Drink 2-3L water daily
  • Avoid: Junk food, fried items, sugary drinks
